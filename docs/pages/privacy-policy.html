Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19.03.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the CDA Project! This Privacy Policy outlines how I collect, use, and protect the data I gather from Discord servers that participate in the Compromised Discord Accounts (CDA) Project. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data Collection

I collect data from Discord servers that have explicitly consented to participate in the CDA Project. The data I collect includes, but is not limited to:

  • Case Number: A unique identifier for each reported case.
  • Found On: The date the compromised account was detected.
  • Found On Server: The name of the Discord server where the compromised account was detected.
  • Discord ID: The unique identifier of the compromised Discord account.
  • Username: The current username of the compromised account.
  • Account Status: The status of the account (e.g., "COMPROMISED").
  • Behaviour: The observed malicious behavior (e.g., "Markdown URL Obfuscation").
  • Attack Method: The method used in the attack (e.g., "Phishing Website").
  • Attack Vector: The technical implementation of the attack (e.g., "Cloned Steam Pages").
  • Attack Goal: The objective of the attack (e.g., "Steam Accounts").
  • Attack Surface: The platform or service targeted by the attack (e.g., "Steam").
  • Suspected Region of Origin: The suspected geographical origin of the attack based on the URL used (if known).
  • Surface URL: The initial URL used in the attack.
  • Surface URL Domain: The domain of the initial URL.
  • Surface URL Status: The status of the initial URL (e.g., "ACTIVE").
  • Final URL: The final destination URL after any redirections.
  • Final URL Domain: The domain of the final URL.
  • Final URL Status: The status of the final URL (e.g., "ACTIVE").
  • Last Check: The timestamp of the last check performed on the account.

3. Use of Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Threat Analysis: To analyze and track malicious activities across Discord servers.
  • Security Enhancements: To improve the security measures of participating servers and protect their members.
  • Research: To conduct research on emerging threats and attack patterns.
  • Public Awareness: To provide insights and reports on malicious activities to the broader community.
  • Prevention & Detection: To enhance the security of Discord servers that use the CDA Monitor Discord bot.

4. Data Sharing

The data collected through the CDA Project is publicly accessible through our website's dashboard and database. However, servers can request to remain anonymous when they join the project or at any point during the collaboration. I respect requests for anonymity and will not disclose the identity of servers that have requested this protection.

The data may also be shared with:

  • Security Researchers: For the purpose of analyzing and mitigating threats.
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law or to prevent illegal activities.
  • Third-Party Services: Such as VirusTotal, for URL validation and threat analysis.

5. Data Retention

I retain the collected data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Data Security

I take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized use, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the data I have collected about your server.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw your consent for data collection at any time.
  • Anonymity: Request that your server's identity remain anonymous in public data.

To exercise these rights, please contact me on Discord at sinewave_.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact me on Discord at sinewave_.
