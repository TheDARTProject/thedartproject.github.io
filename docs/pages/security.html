Project Security

My priority is to ensure the safety of user data and protect the integrity of the system. Security is a shared responsibility, and I value your contributions to keeping the project secure.

Security Policy

I attach great importance to the security of users' data, but I’m human and not infallible. That's why I rely on you, the open source contributors, to inform me about actual and potential security vulnerabilities. Your help ensures that I maintain a safe environment for everyone involved.

While I take every measure to prevent security issues, I understand that new threats emerge regularly. That's why I have a dedicated process in place to address any concerns swiftly. If you believe you have discovered a vulnerability, please follow the instructions below to report it to me.

Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, I ask that you please do not open GitHub issues for these reports, as GitHub issues are publicly accessible. To ensure your report remains confidential and is handled securely, please use the contact method provided below:

  • Email: EMAIL COMING SOON
  • Discord: sinewave_
  • Response time: I guarantee a response within two workdays.
  • Security patches: I will work as quickly as possible to provide a patch and mitigate the vulnerability.

Your cooperation and understanding are highly appreciated as I continue to ensure the safety of our community. Thank you for helping me maintain a secure environment!
