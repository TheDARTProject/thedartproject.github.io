Domain Classification Guide

Platform Categories:

  • Discord/Nitro: Fake Nitro gifts, bots, or tool downloads
  • Gaming: Steam/CS:GO/Valorant item or trade scams
  • Crypto: Fake wallets, tokens, or airdrop campaigns
  • Social/Media: Login pages mimicking Instagram/Facebook
  • Delivery/Apple: USPS/iCloud phishing and notifications

Threat Types:

  • Credential Theft: Fake login pages stealing usernames/passwords
  • Giveaway/Reward: Fake free items, currency, or subscriptions
  • Fake Support: Impersonates official support channels
  • Malware: Tricks users into downloading malicious software
  • Investment Scam: Promises unrealistic crypto returns

Hosting Tactics:

  • Free Hosting: Used to quickly spin up phishing sites
  • Link Shorteners: Obfuscates final destination of links
  • CDN Abuse: Legitimate hosts misused to serve bad content
  • IPFS: Decentralized file storage hard to take down
  • Redirect Chains: Chains of redirects to mask true origin

Phishing Techniques:

  • Typosquatting: Domains that mimic real brands (e.g. paypaI.com)
  • UI Cloning: Pixel-perfect copies of real login screens
  • Popup Overlays: Fake browser popups urging action
  • QR Phishing: QR codes linking to fake login portals
  • Email Spoofing: Fake senders mimicking official addresses
